ROLLBACK

Undo work done (transactional).

Syntax:

   ROLLBACK [WORK] [TO [SAVEPOINT]'savepoint_text_identifier'];

   ROLLBACK [WORK] [FORCE 'force_text'];

FORCE - will manually rollback an in-doubt distributed transaction
